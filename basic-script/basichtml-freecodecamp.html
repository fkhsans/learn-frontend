What's Atom and Molecule?

Atom and molecule is the constituents of matter in the universe. An atom is the basic building block of matter and the smallest unit of a chemical element that retains the properties of that element. It consists of a central nucleus containing positively charged protons and neutral neutrons, surrounded by negatively charged electrons that orbit the nucleus.

A molecule is the smallest unit of a chemical compound or element that retains its specific properties, consisting of two or more atoms chemically bonded together. These atoms can be of the same element, like an oxygen molecule (O₂), or different elements, such as in a water molecule (H₂O). The atoms are held by chemical bonds, most commonly covalent bonds, where electrons are shared.
